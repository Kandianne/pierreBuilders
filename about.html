About

"From Foundation to Finish"

Pierre Builders Inc. has been developing homes, buildings, and structures for over 25 years. Founder, Lucien Pierre, began building in the Caribbean and later moved business to Los Angeles, California in 1989, where it resides today. Lucien and his building team have completed various Southern Californian projects, with a focus on hillside sites. Popular Building locations include: Studio City, Mullholland Dr., Malibu, Hollywood Hills, and Encino.

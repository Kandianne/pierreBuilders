Pierre Builders Inc. is dedicated to working hard to provide you with customized, hands-on service so you can create your dream home from foundation to finish.

We have been developing homes, buildings, and structures for over 25 years. Founder, Lucien Pierre, began building in the Caribbean and later moved business to Los Angeles, California in 1989, where it resides today. Lucien and his building team have completed various Southern Californian projects, with a focus on hillside sites. Popular Building locations include: Studio City, Mullholland Dr., Malibu, Hollywood Hills, and Encino.

We make sure to maintain a hands-on approach, which means we have better control over every area of each specific project and our knowledgeable staff stays aware of changes during the building process. We cooperate with you to provide quality work, that fits your schedule and ideal budget. You have have our full support until the completion of your project and until you are completely satisfied.
